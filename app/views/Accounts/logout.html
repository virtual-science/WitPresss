Welcome to MyRent

Your guide to residential property

The 2004 Residential Tenancies act gives every landlord and tenant a right to a rent review once each year.

The Act makes it illegal for a landlord to charge a rent higher than the market rent.

If there is a dispute about the market rent, either the landlord or the tenant can take a case to the Private Residential Tenancies Board (the PTRB).

The problem with this system is in establishing an objective way of determining the market rent of a property.

The purpose of myrent is to gather information on rents from landlords and tenants and allow any registered user to generate a report on the state of the rental market.

The success of the project will depend on the willingness of tenants and landlords to provide information about their properties.

Log in or create a new account and input your details on the Input Data page. To provide the required information will take just a few minutes of your time.
